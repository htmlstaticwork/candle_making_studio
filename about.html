Our Journey

The Heart of Our Craft

Born from a Flicker of Inspiration.

In 2018, Studio Candle began in a small suburban garage with just three scents and a dream to bring warmth to every home.

Founded by artisan Clara Meadows, our studio has grown into a sanctuary for creators. We believe that candle making is more than a craft—it's a sensory journey that connects us to the present moment and to each other.
